About NoteShred

Created To Scratch An Itch

We've often found ourselves sending sensitive information over the internet on numerous occasions and wished there was a better way. Cheap hotels that wanted our credit card info over email, people who needed our social security numbers, clients who want a password re-issued, parents wanting the password to their cable account
We wanted something easy for ad-hoc use that doesn't require the recipient to sign up to some service in order to receive the information. That was the driving reason behind NoteShred.

Shreddy The Robot

Shreddy the Robot was created by the talented Heroud Ramos (@heroudr)

Shreddy was intended to help give something traditionally complicated and unwelcoming like encryption and internet security a more friendly face and hopefully encourage less technical people to try out NoteShred.
Heroud is available for contract work and you can see more of his work at AdCharacters.com

The Creators

We are based in San Francisco, California although originally from Sydney, Australia.
NoteShred is a side project for us. We believe that not everything in life should cost money and we're happy to offer a free tool for the world to use.

About NoteShred

NoteShred is a service for sending private information over the internet with password protected and encrypted notes.
